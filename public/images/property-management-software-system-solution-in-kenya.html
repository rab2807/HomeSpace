What is a Real Estate Software?

In nutshell a real estate software is a system that helps manage relationships between buyers, sellers, agents, brokers, developers, and financial institutions and caters to the multiple requirements of real estate agents and real estate firms. From managing incoming requests to preparing price quotes, from selling commercial real-estate to property management, the real estate system takes care of the end-to-end process management for real estate agencies, a look at real estate management system features:

  • Accounting & Financial Management
  • Rental Collection Processing
  • Tenant/contact management
  • Real Estate Contract Management
  • Tenant self-service portals
  • Applicant Screening
  • Work Order/Maintenance
  • Mobile Application
  • Reporting & Analytics
  • Prospect/Lead Tracking
  • Unit Inspection Forms and more
